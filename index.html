Odin Recipes

  • Coconut Curry Butternut Squash Soup Very filling and even a little zesty, this butternut squash soup is a decadent appetizer. Butternut squash is hearty on its own, but the coconut milk makes the mixture truly rich. Serve with a warm baguette.
  • Chili-Topped Potato Soup Soup favorites, chunky chili and cheesy-smooth potato soup, balance each other in this marriage of spicy and mild.
  • Christmas Eve Beef Stew This is a family tradition for Christmas Eve! Serve with a green salad and a loaf of warm bread. It can also be made in a slow cooker.
